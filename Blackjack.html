Black Jack

Rules:

- You have to pick multiple cards. Simultaneously the computer will also pick random cards. Score of picked cards will be added and the one whose score will be closer to 21 will win the game.

- If the score exceeds 21 you will BUST. If both players will Bust it would be a tie!

- Ace card has 11 points and picture card carries 10 points. Rest cards carry point with respect to their numbers.

Game will automatically start in 60 seconds...
